- Less crowded market

**Market Size:** Large/Massive
- Everyone with passwords
- Privacy-conscious users
- Business users

**Competitors:**
- Very crowded (Bitwarden, 1Password, LastPass, etc.)

**Monetization:**

**User Journey:**

## üéØ My Final Recommendation

### **Pivot to Password Manager (with Text Encryption as Differentiator)**

**Reasoning:**

1. **Market Reality:**
   - Password managers = $2B+ market
   - Text encryption = niche market
   - You can't ignore the larger opportunity

2. **Natural Progression:**
   - Password generator ‚Üí Password storage (logical)
   - Users already generate passwords, why not store them?
   - Text encryption becomes "bonus feature"

3. **Competitive Advantage:**
   - "Local-only password manager" = clear positioning
   - "That also encrypts text" = unique differentiator
   - Few competitors offer both

4. **Monetization:**
   - Password managers have proven premium models
   - Text encryption alone is harder to monetize
   - Hybrid approach = best of both worlds

5. **User Journey:**
   - Password manager = daily use = high engagement
   - Text encryption = occasional use = low engagement
   - Daily use = better retention = better monetization
