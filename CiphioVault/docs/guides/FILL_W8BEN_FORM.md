# Fill Out Form W-8BEN

**Location:** App Store Connect → Agreements → Tax Forms → Form W-8BEN

**What it is:** U.S. tax form for foreign individuals receiving payments from Apple

---

## Part I: Identification of Beneficial Owner

### 1. Name of Individual (Pre-filled)
✅ **"Kaan Gul"** - Already filled, correct

---

### 2. Country or Region of Citizenship
**What to enter:**
- ✅ **"Türkiye"** or **"Turkey"**
- Select from dropdown

**Action:** Select "Türkiye" from dropdown

---

### 3. Permanent Residence Address (Pre-filled)
✅ **Already filled:**
```
Cevatpasa mah. Selanik sok.
Istanbul Bayrampasa 34045
Türkiye
```

**Action:** ✅ Verify it's correct (should be fine)

---

### 4. Mailing Address (Pre-filled)
✅ **Already filled:** Same as permanent residence

**Action:** ✅ Leave as is (correct)

---

### 5. U.S. Taxpayer Identification Number
**Options:**
- EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- SSN (Social Security Number)

**What to do:**
- ✅ **Leave blank** (you don't have a U.S. tax ID)
- ✅ Click "Don't have a U.S. Tax ID?" if there's a link
- ❌ **Don't enter anything** - you're not a U.S. taxpayer

**Action:** ✅ Leave both fields blank

---

### 6.a. Foreign Tax Identifying Number
**What it is:** Your Turkish tax ID number (if you have one)

**What to enter:**
- ✅ **Leave blank** (optional for individuals)
- ✅ Or enter your Turkish tax ID if you have one

**Note:** Not required for individuals, but you can add it if you want

**Action:** ✅ Leave blank (optional)

---

### 7. Reference Number(s)
**What it is:** Optional reference number

**What to enter:**
- ✅ **Leave blank** (not required)

**Action:** ✅ Leave blank

---

### 8. Date of Birth
**What it is:** Your date of birth (required)

**What to enter:**
- ✅ **Your date of birth** in format: MM/DD/YYYY
- Example: 01/15/1990

**Action:** ✅ Enter your date of birth

---

## Part II: Claim of Tax Treaty Benefits

### 9. Tax Treaty Certification
**What it says:**
> "I certify that the beneficial owner is a resident of Türkiye within the meaning of the income tax treaty between United States and that country or region."

**What to do:**
- ✅ **Check "Yes"** or select "Yes"
- This confirms you're a Turkish resident under the U.S.-Turkey tax treaty

**Why:** The U.S. and Türkiye have a tax treaty that prevents double taxation

**Action:** ✅ Select/Check "Yes"

---

### 10. Special Rates and Conditions
**What it is:** Special tax treaty provisions (usually not needed)

**What to enter:**
- ✅ **Leave blank** (standard treaty applies)
- ✅ The standard U.S.-Turkey tax treaty rate applies automatically

**If you see fields:**
- **Article and paragraph:** Leave blank
- **Rate of withholding:** Leave blank
- **Type of income:** Leave blank
- **Additional conditions:** Leave blank

**Action:** ✅ Leave all fields blank (standard treaty applies)

---

## Important Notes

### ✅ Standard Form
- All non-U.S. developers complete this
- Required for receiving payments
- Standard U.S. tax compliance

### ✅ Tax Treaty Benefits
- U.S.-Turkey tax treaty prevents double taxation
- You pay taxes in Türkiye (not U.S.)
- No U.S. tax withholding (usually)

### ✅ No U.S. Tax ID Needed
- Foreign individuals don't need U.S. tax ID
- Leave those fields blank
- Your Turkish tax ID is optional

## Common Questions

### Q: Do I need a U.S. Tax ID?
**A:** No, foreign individuals don't need a U.S. tax ID. Leave those fields blank.

### Q: What's my Foreign Tax ID?
**A:** Your Turkish tax identification number (if you have one). It's optional - you can leave it blank.

### Q: What if I don't know the tax treaty details?
**A:** That's fine - just check "Yes" for line 9 and leave line 10 blank. The standard treaty applies automatically.

### Q: What date format for Date of Birth?
**A:** MM/DD/YYYY (e.g., 01/15/1990)

### Q: Can I submit without Date of Birth?
**A:** No, Date of Birth is required. You must enter it.
