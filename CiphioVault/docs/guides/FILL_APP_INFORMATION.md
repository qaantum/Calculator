## Important Notes

### Name Guidelines
- **18 characters max** (including spaces)
- "Ciphio Vault" = 13 characters ✅
- Can't change after first submission (hard to change later)
- Choose carefully!

### Subtitle Guidelines
- **30 characters max**
- Appears below app name on App Store
- Can be changed later
- Use keywords for SEO

### Category Guidelines
- **Primary category** is most important for discovery
- **Utilities** is standard for password managers
- Can change later, but affects search ranking

### Age Ratings
- **Must be accurate** - Apple reviews this
- Password managers are always **4+**
- No objectionable content = lowest rating
