## Common Questions

### Q: Do I need to pay U.S. taxes?
**A:** No, as a Turkish developer, you pay taxes in Türkiye. This form just confirms your foreign status.

### Q: What if I don't have a title?
**A:** Use "Owner" - it's the simplest and correct for individual developers.

### Q: Can I change this later?
**A:** Yes, you can update tax forms anytime in App Store Connect.

### Q: What if I have employees in the U.S.?
**A:** Then you'd need to check "No" for that part, but as an individual developer, you don't have U.S. employees.

### Q: What if I'm not the beneficial owner?
**A:** If someone else will receive payments, you need to update the form. But for individual developers, you are the beneficial owner.
